Hi, I am Simon Ximeng Liu!

Ph.D., IEEE Member, CCF Member

Professor in Fuzhou University, Research Fellow in Singapore Management University

Email: snbnix AT gmail.com

My research areas focus on applied cryptography, cloud security, big data security, privacy-preserving data mining/machine learning techniques, and network security. Since 2013, I have already published over 100 research articles in both journals and conferences, such as IEEE Transactions on Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, IEEE Transactions on Computers, IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing, IEEE Transactions on Service Computing, IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing, IET information security, etc
